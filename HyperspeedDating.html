Hyperspeed Dating Overview

Gameplay

"Hyper Speed Dating With Totally Normal Characters" is a dating sim featuring limited time interactions and uncanny characters. This game was developed during the Unwrap game jam by a team of six, including two character artists, one 3D artist for backgrounds, one musician, and two programmers, myself being one of the programmers.

Main takeaways

Game design

The theme of the jam was "Keep it moving," which inspired us to create a speed dating concept. We decided to take it to the extreme by giving players only 15 seconds to get to know each prospect. To add to the fun, we also designed some goofy, unconventional characters.

Transitions & effects

In addition to some extra programming, my main focus was on enhancing the game's feel by adding "juice", such as smooth transitions, appealing animations, and engaging effects.
