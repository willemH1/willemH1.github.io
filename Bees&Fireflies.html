Bees & Fireflies Overview

Gameplay

"Bees & Fireflies" is a side-scrolling clean 'em up game where you guide a cheerful bee spreading magical pollen to heal the forest's infection. It was my first game jam, and I had the pleasure of collaborating with a talented person I met online. He hand-painted all the art and scanned it in, while I took care of the programming, implementation and some soundFX. You can see more of his amazing work at Humane Tiger .

Main takeaways

Collaboration

It was my first time collaborating with another person on a game. I found it important to keep my teammate up to date at all times on my progress, so I sent him videos and pictures every couple of hours. That way, our collaboration was very streamlined, and we were always on the same page.

Rapid prototyping

I not only focused on quickly prototyping gameplay but also on important tasks like deploying to WebGL and Itch, which I had no experience with. By tackling these tasks early in the game jam, we had plenty of time to fix bugs and unexpected behavior that arose.
