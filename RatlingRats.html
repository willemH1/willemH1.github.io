Ratling Rats Overview

Gameplay

"Ratling Rats" is an action game inspired by Pikmin, where you command a rat rebellion to defend your base from walking mousetraps. This game was developed in a 2-day game jam with a 4 person team: two artists and a fellow programmer. My roles included additional programming, sound design, and VFX. Check out the other programmer, Jonathon, and our lead artist, Ziqqy.

Main takeaways

3D game jam

It was the first time I ever made a 3D game for a game jam. I think we underestimated the amount of extra work a 3D game requires to look good. From the lighting and camera clipping to the textures and models, 3D is a lot of work to get right, especially in only 2 days. But still I'm proud of what we were able to achieve in such a short time span.

Enemy Behaviour

The enemies and player minions navigate the map using a navmesh. I had used it before and found it quite easy to set up. However, we ran into an issue with elevated surfaces.
